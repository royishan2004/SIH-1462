School Safety Programmes(by NDRF/SDRF)

NDMA in partnership with the 22 States/UTs implemented ‘National School Safety Programme (NSSP)’, in 8600 schools in 43 districts spread over 22 States/UTs of the country falling in seismic zone IV & V with the aim to sensitize children and the school community on disaster preparedness and safety measures.

Under the project, following activities were undertaken:

  • Formulation of National Disaster Management Guidelines on School Safety Policy, 2016.
  • Preparation of Teachers Training Module on School Safety by NIDM.
  • Translation into regional language and printing of Teacher’s Training Modules.
  • Training of Trainers Programme (15 trainers per district).
  • Training of teachers (500 teachers, officials etc per district).
  • Preparation of School Disaster Management Plans in 200 school in each 43 districts.
  • Review and approval of School DM Plans prepared by 200 schools in each of 43 districts.
  • Disaster Preparedness Kits in 200 schools in each of 43 districts.
  • Mock drills in 200 schools in each of 43 districts.
  • Translation, printing, dissemination of IEC Material.
  • Sensitization Programmes at State/UT level.
  • Grant-in-aid to State Education Departments for sensitization programme and awareness activities.
  • Rapid Visual Survey in 200 schools in each of 43 districts.
  • Non-Structural Mitigation Measures in 200 schools in each of 43 districts.
  • Structural Retrofitting of one school each in 22 States/UTs.
